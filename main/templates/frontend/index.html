About
The DLC

The Dean’s list council was established in 1998. The members of the List Council are high-profile students, distinct and successful in their studies and thus meriting recognition by the Dean. The main goals of the Council are to create a sense of effective competition among students, encourage honor students to excel in CBA, build a stronger relationship between faculty, administration and students and -above all -provide suggestions, opinions and complaints of honor students about academic issues and faculty, important feedback in continuous improvement at CBA.
