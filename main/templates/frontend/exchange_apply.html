World-Class Accreditation

AACSB Accredited Excellence

The Kuwait University College of Business Administration is proudly accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB International), the longest-serving global accrediting body for business schools. This prestigious accreditation places KU CBA among the top 5% of business schools worldwide.

Global Recognition: Accepted worldwide
Quality Assurance: Rigorous standards
Academic Excellence: Top-tier faculty
Global Network: Elite partnerships

AACSB accreditation ensures that your exchange experience at KU CBA meets the highest international standards for business education. Credits earned here are recognized and valued by institutions worldwide.

For KU Students

Study Abroad as a KU Student

Expand your academic horizons by studying at one of our partner universities around the world.

Eligibility

  • Must be a current KU CBA student
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Completed at least 60 credit hours
  • Good academic standing

Application Timeline

  • Fall Exchange: Apply by March 1st
  • Spring Exchange: Apply by September 1st
  • Processing takes 4-6 weeks
  • Semester or full year options

Required Documents

  • Official KU transcript
  • English proficiency proof
  • Letter of motivation
  • Academic recommendation letter

Ready to Study Abroad?

Visit the Exchange Program Office in the College of Business Administration for detailed application guidelines and personalized advising.
